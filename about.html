Discover Kenya

Explore the Heart of Africa with Allekey - Tours and Travel

Kenya is a land of breathtaking contrasts—from golden savannahs and snow-capped mountains to pristine beaches and lush forests. At Travela, we craft premium experiences that take you deep into the soul of this beautiful country.

Whether you're seeking an unforgettable safari, an exclusive beach holiday, or a scenic mountain retreat, our luxury packages are designed to immerse you in the magic of Kenya, with personalized service every step of the way.

Masai Mara National Reserve

Witness the Great Wildebeest Migration and enjoy world-class game drives in Kenya’s most iconic safari destination.

Diani Beach

Relax on white sandy beaches with crystal clear waters, luxury resorts, and thrilling water activities on the Kenyan coast.

Mount Kenya

Climb Africa’s second-highest peak, explore alpine forests, and discover breathtaking mountain scenery.

Amboseli National Park

Famous for its large herds of elephants and stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro across the border in Tanzania.

Lake Nakuru National Park

A bird lover’s paradise with thousands of flamingos, rhinos, and panoramic viewpoints.

Hell’s Gate National Park

A dramatic landscape of cliffs and gorges, perfect for hiking, cycling, and exploring geothermal wonders.

Tsavo East & West

One of the largest wildlife sanctuaries in the world with rugged terrain, lava flows, and stunning wildlife encounters.

Lamu Island

Step back in time in this UNESCO World Heritage site, rich with Swahili culture, dhow cruises, and historic architecture.

Nairobi City Experience

Enjoy urban adventures in Kenya’s capital—from the Giraffe Centre to vibrant nightlife and fine dining.

Dickyways Tours & Travels is your trusted partner for unforgettable adventures across Kenya and beyond. Explore with ease, travel with purpose.
